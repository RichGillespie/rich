Richard Gillespie

Electronic Music Producer & DJ

Richard Gillespie is an electronic music producer who has been involved with making music since the 1990's. Richard has recorded and performed in a wide range of genres but currently chooses to produce music in the deep & organic house genres. In addition to producing music, Richard started to DJ in 2017. Stay tuned for new music and DJ sets from Richard Gillespie in the years to come.
