Richard Gillespie

Electronic Music Producer & DJ

Richard Gillespie is an electronic music producer who has been involved with making music since the 1990's. Richard has performed and recorded in a wide range of genres ranging from Reggae to Jazz but is currently focused on producing electronic music. He prefers using Ableton Live as his DAW of choice to create. Listeners can look forward to many new releases from Richard in the years to come.
